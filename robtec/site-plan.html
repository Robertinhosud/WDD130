I believe that the number one question that my website should answer is what is the quality of my service, this will be done through the images of the projects that will be on the site • What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site? I think that the activity that will be carried out on my site will be publicity and relationships with clients and potential clients, all my data to be hired and my work will be there • What information is necessary to complete any actions your site offers? What is a website? I can tell you one thing: if you have a company and you don't yet have a website, you're way behind the competition! In this article you'll learn about this topical subject that will bring more visibility to your business. Website is a combination of the words web (network) and site (site), meaning that a website is a place on the web. It is the word used to define the place where one or more pages are available to be accessed via the Internet. There are some basic elements that a website usually has. We'll explain them from the point of view of a business, but they are fully applicable to other causes, such as: Personal blog Non-profit organizations News portals Official pages Event pages To give you an example, imagine a physical store as a website, or just a website for short. The entrance to the store is the main page of the site, also called the Home page. The counter can be seen as a contact page, where the customer can talk to the store. The shelves are a page for displaying products, with sub-pages for each category of object. Can you understand a bit more? This was a very basic example to help you, but a website can go much further than that. Pages are customizable and adjustable according to the needs of each person or company. Now visualize your business like this, and imagine how many people can visit your company online. According to data from Globo's G1 news portal, the 2018 ICT Households survey shows that 126.9 million Brazilians use the internet. Imagine today? That number is only growing. This proves the importance of companies having an online presence, regardless of their size or area of activity. Python Projects

The origin of Python Launched in 1991, Python was created by Dutch mathematician and programmer Guido van Rossum, who at the time was working at Centrum Wiskunde & Informatica (CWI) in Amsterdam. Interestingly, Python was named after the British comedy series Monty Python's Flying Circus. According to van Rossum, he was looking for a short and mysterious name, and "Python" fit the bill. What is Python? Python is a programming language. In general, programming languages are responsible for communication between computers and human beings. They allow programs to be created to physically and logically control a machine, based on a series of instructions, symbols, keywords, etc. If you need to create a program or optimize a task, we can help.
